Descriptions

Table 1:

1. Game: This column lists the names or identifiers of the games.

2. Total Kills: This column shows the total number of kills that occurred in each game.

3. Numbers of Players: This column indicates the number of players who participated in each game.

4. Best Player: This column identifies the player who had the best performance in terms of kills in each game.

5. Player With Most Deaths: This column shows the player who had the most deaths in each game.

Table 2:

The best player was defined by surviving a match, that is, dying less during all matches. You can view the metrics via graph and table.

Table 3:

The player who killed the most was defined by the player who killed the most in all matches. You can view the metrics via table.
